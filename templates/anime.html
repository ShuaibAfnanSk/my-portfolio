Our Services

Collaborative Filtering

Recommend anime based on user behavior and similarities with other users, utilizing collective data to provide personalized suggestions.

Content Based Recommendation

Suggest anime based on user preferences and characteristics of the content, focusing on similarities in genres, themes, and styles.

Popularity Based Recommendation

Suggest popular anime titles based on overall popularity metrics, such as ratings, reviews, and view counts.

Mood & Genre Based Recommendation

Recommend anime on basis of users current mood and also users favorite genre.
